Nikola Jokić

About Me

I’m Nikola Jokić, a professional basketball player from Serbia and a two-time NBA MVP. I play for the Denver Nuggets, where I focus on making smart plays, passing, and helping my team win. People call me "The Joker" because of my unselfish style and unique approach to the game. I believe skill and basketball IQ matter more than athleticism, and I try to prove that every time I step on the court. Off the court, I enjoy horse racing and spending time with my family. For me, basketball is about having fun and playing the right way.

My Career

2018-19

First NBA All-Star selection. Led Denver to the playoffs. Averaged 20.1 points, 10.8 rebounds, and 7.3 assists.

2019-20

Western Conference Finals appearance. Maintained strong all-around play with 19.9 points, 9.7 rebounds, and 7.0 assists.

2020-21

First MVP award. Career-high averages of 26.4 points, 10.8 rebounds, and 8.3 assists.

2021-22

Back-to-back MVP winner. Dominant statistical output—27.1 points, 13.8 rebounds, and 7.9 assists.

2022-23

NBA champion and Finals MVP. Near triple-double season with 24.5 points, 11.8 rebounds, and 9.8 assists.

2023-24

Career-high 56-point game. Third MVP award. Averaged 29.8 points, 12.6 rebounds, and 10.2 assists.
